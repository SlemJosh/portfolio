About Me

It started as a passion for gaming. Ever since my first computer in 1996, I wanted to be on it all the time gaming. As I grew more familiar with the internet and all it had to offer, it only begged for more attention from me. Whilst in highschool even looked at procuring some of the skills to turn this box of joy into a box of productivity. Stumbled along the way and again in college looked at getting more of those skills to pay the bills. Life once again interrupted, but not before I had a chance to really sample some of the basics of what makes up a computer science degree. Now I'm fully invested in a coding bootcamp, where I can focus on the work itself and finally fulfill my goal of becoming a software developer/engineer in the near future.
